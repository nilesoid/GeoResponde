# Project Charter v1.0

## GeoResponde Venezuela

**Open Geospatial Information Hub for the 2026 Venezuela Earthquake**

**Version:** 1.0
**Status:** Draft
**Date:** June 2026

---

# 1. Mission

GeoResponde Venezuela is an open, independent, bilingual initiative that centralizes, organizes, and visualizes reliable geospatial and humanitarian information related to the 2026 Venezuela earthquake.

The project exists to help affected communities, volunteers, humanitarian organizations, researchers, journalists, and decision makers quickly find trustworthy information without duplicating the work already being carried out by official institutions and community initiatives.

Our role is to connect information, not replace it.

---

# 2. Vision

Become the primary geospatial information hub for the Venezuela earthquake Responde by integrating authoritative datasets, humanitarian resources, and scientific products into a single open platform.

---

# 3. Core Principles

## Accuracy First

Information is more valuable than speed.

Whenever possible, every dataset must indicate:

* Source
* Update time
* License
* Confidence level

---

## Do Not Duplicate

GeoResponde does not replace existing initiatives.

Instead, it indexes, organizes and references them.

Examples:

✓ Missing persons portals

✓ Official situation reports

✓ Humanitarian organizations

✓ Scientific repositories

---

## Open by Default

Whenever licensing allows, all datasets should be available through open formats.

Preferred formats include:

* GeoJSON
* PMTiles
* Vector Tiles
* COG
* GeoPackage
* CSV
* JSON

---

## Neutrality

GeoResponde is politically neutral.

The platform does not publish opinions, political statements or unverified claims.

Its purpose is technical and humanitarian.

---

## Transparency

Every map layer must answer:

* Where does this information come from?
* When was it updated?
* Who maintains it?
* Can it be downloaded?

---

# 6. Out of Scope

GeoResponde will not:

* Maintain missing persons databases
* Publish casualty lists
* Replace official emergency agencies
* Host rumors or unverified reports
* Act as a news organization

---

# 7. Information Confidence

Each dataset will receive one confidence level.

🟢 Verified

Official source or independently verified.

🟡 Cross-Referenced

Multiple independent sources agree.

🟠 Community Report

Single community source.

⚪ Pending Verification

Awaiting validation.
